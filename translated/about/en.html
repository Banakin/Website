Why did we create the Faithful organization?

Faithful was launched on November 5, 2020, to create a set of community-based resource packs based on mutual help, following the controversy created by the person who unofficially took over Faithful. Faithful is a do-over of Faithful, but presents the same objectives with different ideologies.


What does Faithful mean?

Although this name sounds a bit strange, it is synonymous with the word "Faithful" which we cannot use. Faithful means close to the same thing while also sounding reasonably good, so we chose that. The name is meant to show that the resource packs conform to the default textures of Minecraft.


What are our goals?

The goal of Faithful is to create textures respecting the basic ideology of default Minecraft textures, without adding too much colour or distorting their original shapes. We try to only add extra details when they are absolutely necessary for making textures.


What are the Faithful projects?

Currently, Faithful is split into six different projects:

  • Faithful 32x: a 32x version of the Minecraft resource pack.
  • Faithful 64x: a 64x version of the Minecraft resource pack.
  • Faithful Mods: 32x texture support for many mods and modpacks.
  • Faithful Dungeons: a mod providing 32x textures for Minecraft Dungeons.
  • Faithful Add-Ons: many additional smaller resource pack on top of 32x or 64x to change your game experience.
  • Faithful Tweaks: essentially like add-ons, but only with small tweaks.


Who can participate to the project?

Anyone can contribute to one or more of our resource packs. There are several Discord servers dedicated to each project and the process of pushing textures to 32x and 64x is automated, but submissions must respect our guidelines to keep a consistent pack.
Regarding third-party projects such as Faithful Dungeons or Faithful Mods, more information is available on their respective Discord servers.
